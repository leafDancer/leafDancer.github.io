About

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science at Peking University, affiliated with the Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies (CFCS). I am also a research intern at the Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI).

My research interests include multi-agent reinforcement learning, computational economics, and algorithmic game theory. I am supervised by Prof. Xiaotie Deng and co-advised by Prof. Bo Li and Prof. Yaodong Yang.
