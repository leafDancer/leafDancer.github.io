About

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computer Science at Peking University, affiliated with the Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies (CFCS). My research interests include multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL), algorithmic game theory (AGT), and quantitative macroeconomics, and I also develop AI tools for social science. I am supervised by Prof. Xiaotie Deng and co-advised by Prof. Bo Li and Prof. Yaodong Yang.
